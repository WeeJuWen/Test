Our Programs

Sutra Study Classes

Regular classes studying the Pure Land sutras, including the Amitabha Sutra and Infinite Life Sutra.

Meditation Sessions

Guided meditation sessions focusing on mindfulness, breathing exercises, and Pure Land visualization.

Chanting Practice

Daily chanting sessions of the name of Amitabha Buddha and traditional Buddhist liturgies.

Weekend Retreats

Periodic retreats for intensive practice, Dharma talks, and community building.

Beginner's Course

Introduction to Pure Land Buddhism for those new to the practice.

Daily Services

Morning and evening services with chanting, meditation, and Dharma readings.
